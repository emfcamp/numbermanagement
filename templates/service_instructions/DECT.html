To use your DECT phone, please follow these instructions carefully.

Register your handset on the network

  1. Go to "Register Handset" or similar in your handset's menu.
    • For example in the Gigaset A170 its Menu> Settings > Handset > Register HS
  2. If asked, enter the System PIN: 0000
  3. You may see "Handset Registered" or similar.

IMPORTANT: DO NOT attemt to register the handset again

You must now do the following test before continuing

Test your Handset's registered to our DECT network with a temporary number.

  1. Dial 90201
    • Beware: This first call may take a whole 30s to connect
  2. If the call connects:
    • You should hear your temporary number read back at you.
    • Continue to the activation section below
  3. If the call fails:
    • Dial 90201 again, and wait 30 whole seconds for the call to connect.
    • If that still doesn't work, please wait 30 minutes and then try the test call again.
    • If the test call does not work after 30 minutes, please visit the Phones tent (POC) 0900-1800 and we'll try to help.

Activate {{number.value}} on your handset

Option 1:

  1. Dial: 900 {{number.value}} {{number.param}} (this is one continuous number)
  2. Wait for confirmation that it has been applied successfully

Option 2:

  1. Dial 900
  2. When asked, enter your number: {{number.value}}
  3. When asked, enter your activation code: {{number.param}}
  4. Wait for confirmation that it has been applied successfuly
